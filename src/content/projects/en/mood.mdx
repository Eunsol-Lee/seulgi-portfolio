## Challenge

MOOD Beauty's first color cosmetics line: a 12-shade lip color collection. Each shade was defined to represent a distinct mood through its name and color, with product design to match.

## Process

A slim, angular metal tube was chosen as the base form. The 12 shades are identified by a colored ring at the base of each tube, making the shade immediately recognizable even with the cap closed. Matte aluminum and glossy resin finishes were applied differently according to shade family, designing the experience down to the fingertips.

## Result

Seven of twelve shades sold out in the first week. The #MoodLip hashtag spread organically on social media, and the collection was selected as Color Item of the Year, leading to a department store pop-up.
