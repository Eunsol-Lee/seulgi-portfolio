## Challenge

Container and package design for DALBIT Cosmetics' first premium skincare line launch. The six-product line (toner, serum, cream, eye cream, sunscreen, cleanser) needed to express a serene, nature-inspired beauty through a consistent design language.

## Process

Starting from the keywords "moonlight" and "botanicals," I defined the formal language of the vessels. Softly rounded shoulders and a subtle pearl-finish surface were designed to evoke moonlight, while translucent labels allow the product's own color to show through. Packaging was finished with silver foil embossing on recyclable kraft boxes.

## Result

Online pre-orders exceeded 2,000 units in the first month of launch. The line was placed in five major beauty select shops domestically, and 82% of purchasers reported that packaging design influenced their buying decision.
