## Challenge

Design for HANEUL Beauty's first cushion foundation compact. A refill cartridge system allows the case body to be used semi-permanently, with the goal of creating a product where sustainable materials and sensory texture coexist.

## Process

A proprietary material blending recycled ABS and bio-resin was developed for the case. The rough-yet-smooth surface texture provides a differentiated tactile experience when held. Refill replacement uses a quarter-turn mechanism at the base, designed so anyone can easily swap cartridges without tools.

## Result

Refill cartridge repurchase rate reached 68% within six months of launch. The product received Green Product certification from the Ministry of Environment and was selected as Product of the Year in the sustainable beauty category.
