## Challenge

Package design for SOUL Fragrance's first signature perfume line. The client's philosophy was that a perfume bottle should transcend being a mere container to become an art object on a shelf. The entire system was designed around a sculptural glass bottle.

## Process

I believed that the weight and coldness of a perfume bottle in the hand is where the fragrance experience begins. Thick crystal glass concentrates weight at the base, while the top narrows to create a silhouette that wraps around the hand. The box uses only silk-screened logo on matte black stock, maximizing the bottle's presence the moment it's revealed.

## Result

The initial production run sold out completely within three months of launch. Over 20 domestic and international beauty media outlets spotlighted the packaging design, leading to commissions for seasonal limited editions.
